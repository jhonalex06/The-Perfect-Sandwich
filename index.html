Ingredients

  • 2 Ripe Mango
  • 2 Cans Condensed Milk 11 ounces
  • 2 Cans Evaporated Milk 11 ounces
  • Sweetener (as your like)
  • 1/2 Cup Lime
  • 6 Rolls Cookies

Equipment

  • Glasses or mold to serve
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Blender
  • Refrigerator
  • Your list icons

Instructions

Step 1. Milk Mixture

  • Place the condensed and evaporated milk in a blender with the 1/2 Cup of Lime
  • Blend

If you wanna you can add sweetener too before blend.

Step 2. Assembling of three layers

Take the cookies out of the wrapper and place on a plate for easy access.

Chop the mangos in little slices.

  • Put a layer of cookies in each glass or mold to serve (individual portion)
  • To create the second layer in the same cup, pour 1/2 cup of the milk mixture and spread around.
  • Third layer consist in a top with the fresh mangos slices.

If you wanna you can add sweetener too before blend.

Step 3. Freeze
